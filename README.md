# <div align="center"><img src="https://atomicpink.com/wp-content/uploads/vintage-decor-logo.png" align="center" width="280px" alt="Vintage Gallery"></div>

<img src="modicon.png" align="left" width="64px"/> Adds a wide selection of new wallpapers in several styles for use in your interior design builds.

I will be adding continuously to this mod, so check for updated often!

Each wallpaper is unique and was created by myself.  I wanted to add more color, vibrancy, and variety to my interior builds, and this is the result!  Included are 45 individual, seamless wallpapers in 5 distinct style groups: Plain, Food, Stripes, Manor, Estate

Most of the pigment powders are created by taking [lime](https://wiki.vintagestory.at/Lime) (ground chalk stones) in multiples of 4 and putting them in a [barrel](https://wiki.vintagestory.at/Barrel) with liquid [dye](https://wiki.vintagestory.at/Dye).  After 4 hours, it will be converted into pigment powder matching the color of the dye. The conversion rate is 1 to 1, so if you put in 12 lime, you will get 12 pigment powder. 

A few pigment powders can made by combining two pigment powders to output a third, new color.  Beige is an example of this.

See your handbook for all pigment powder recipes!

Recipes for the Pink Pigment Powder

### Stencils

Stencils are used to control which wallpaper group your final wallpaper will be from. Stencils are reusable in recipes, much like blueprints. Stencils require 3 [parchment](https://wiki.vintagestory.at/Parchment) or [linen](https://wiki.vintagestory.at/Linen) (any), plus 1 [stick](https://wiki.vintagestory.at/Stick) and 1 of [coal](https://wiki.vintagestory.at/Coal) (any).  Different placements of the [stick](https://wiki.vintagestory.at/Stick) and charcoal will create different stencils.  See your handbook for specific shaped crafting recipes!

<img src="https://www.atomicpink.com/wp-content/uploads/vintagedecor-crafting-a-stencil.png" width="250"><br>
Recipe for the Food wallpaper group's stencil

### Wallpapers

Wallpapers are crafted in the grid using [parchment](https://wiki.vintagestory.at/Parchment) (or [linen](https://wiki.vintagestory.at/Linen)), a stencil, and pigment powders. Each batch of wallpaper requires 32 [parchment](https://wiki.vintagestory.at/Parchment) or [linen](https://wiki.vintagestory.at/Linen) (any), your desired stencil, and 2 pigment powders. See your handbook for all combinations and styles.

## How to get some new wallpaper
- Vanilla traders: Artisan, villager-wall, luxuries, and furniture traders can sell (and some buy) the new wallpapers.
- Tradie (optional): The Tradie Painter sells the full set when the mod is loaded.
- Craft them yourself!

## Compatibility notes
- Patch-only mod; no vanilla assets are replaced, and optional integrations are conditionally applied so missing mods will not log warnings.
- Safe for servers/clients; clients need the mod for textures and names.

## Installation
1) Download the latest release (or the release of your choice) and drop it into your `VintagestoryData/Mods` directory.
2) Launch or restart the game. Load order can be anywhere; place after [Traders Expansion (Tradie)](https://mods.vintagestory.at/tradie) if you're using it.
